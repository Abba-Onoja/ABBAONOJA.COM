Regression · Analysis 2025

Tip Prediction
Using ML Models

A practice project built to get hands-on with regression concepts using the classic Seaborn tips dataset. The goal was straightforward: can you predict how much someone will tip based on their bill, party size, day of the week, and whether they smoke? Five models were trained and compared — including Gradient Boosting with GridSearchCV hyperparameter tuning — to see which one reads the table best.

Visualisation · Finance 2026

Compound Interest
Calculator

A Streamlit web app for comparing two investment scenarios side by side. Built as a deep dive into Streamlit's reactive state system and Plotly's interactive charting. The app handles varying compounding frequencies — daily, monthly, annually — and calculates an effective rate per payment period when contribution schedules and compounding intervals differ. Includes a growth line chart, an annual interest histogram, and a CSV export.

Service Gap Finder
for Abuja

A lot of residential areas in Abuja — particularly the satellite towns — lack access to basic everyday services like laundry and last-mile logistics. This project maps those gaps. It identifies neighbourhoods where residents have to travel unreasonable distances to access a simple service, using publicly available location data and proximity analysis. The goal is a clear, visual picture of where service demand exists but supply does not.

Agricultural Policy
Monitor

Nigerian agricultural policy is scattered across government portals that most people never read.
